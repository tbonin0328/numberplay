Welcome!

Dr. Areste provides a wide range of services within the scope of her practice as a Naturopathic Family Physician. The primary modality Dr. Areste uses is NeuroModulation Technique (NMT). Dr. Areste also utilizes modalities such as diet, exercise, auricular therapy, counseling, dietary supplements and low level laser therapy to complement NMT. She also provides treatments specifically focused on addressing Wilson's Temperature Syndrome. Dr. Areste is a graduate of Bastyr University and a licensed Naturopathic Physician.

Dr. Areste's goal is to educate and empower her patients and facilitate the healing process. She takes a direct approach to healing by dealing with the roots of health problems. Dr. Areste focuses on NMT due to its effectiveness at working on the causes of poor health and its ability to help reduce immediate discomfort. Dr. Areste treats people of all ages and treats a variety of conditions, both acute and chronic.
