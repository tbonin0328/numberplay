New Patients

Information to help you prepare for your first visit is provided below.

Office Visits: Initial and Ongoing

Dr. Areste takes a holistic approach to every patient she sees. Initial office visits usually last 1.5 hours and require completion of the following forms (you can access all forms in one PDF by clicking on "New Patients" and then "Forms" in the menu above):

  • Release Authorization
  • Health Insurance Form
  • Medical History
  • Registration
  • Review of Systems
  • Pyroluria Questionnaire
  • Amino Acid Therapy Chart

To get the most out of your first visit, please print these forms, complete them, and bring them with you . Dr. Areste will discuss your history with you and use this information to develop a working diagnosis and treatment plan. Further office visits usually last up to 45 minutes. You will receive your first NMT treatment at the time of the initial visit.

Billing and Insurance

Our office bills insurance companies with whom we are contracted. If your insurance coverage is denied, or it covers Naturopathic care but is not one of our contracted insurance companies, you will be responsible for payment in full and for billing your company for reimbursement. You are also responsible for payment of all deductibles, co-payments, co-insurances, and other non-covered services at the time of service.

Appointments/Cancellation Fee

Our office will work with you to make appointments that fit your schedule. We ask that you arrive on time so other patients are not inconvenienced. Please be aware, if you are late for an appointment, your treatment may be abbreviated. We require 24-hour notice by phone or voice mail for cancellations or changes. A "No Show" fee of $85.00 will be charged for all appointments not canceled 24 hours in advance.
