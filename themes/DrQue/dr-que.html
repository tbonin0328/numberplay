Personal Statement

Since graduating from Bastyr University and becoming a licensed Naturopathic Physician, I have practiced various forms of energy medicine. The focus of my practice is NeuroModulation Technique because it is the most effective and flexible treatment I have used. To complement this focus, I include other modalities such as diet, exercise, Buteyko Breathing, auricular therapy, counseling, education, dietary supplements and low level laser therapy. I treat people of all ages for a variety of conditions, both acute and chronic. I also provide services specifically focused on treating Wilson's Temperature Syndrome.

My goals are to educate and empower the people who seek my help. I work to facilitate your healing process by dealing with the roots of health problems. My strong focus on NMT stems from its effectiveness at working on the causes of poor health and its ability to help reduce immediate discomfort.

I continue my training as a Naturopathic Physician and NMT practitioner by attending seminars and conferences, participating in web support programs, and reading several journals.
